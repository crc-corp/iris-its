Vicon Camera Control Code

Commands sent to a camera remain active until another command is received. For example, a pan will not stop until a command is sent with pan speed of zero. There is a timeout function, but it is well over 30 seconds.

For certain receivers, it appears that auxiliary functions require an extended packet. For these receivers, a basic command packet with aux functions set will have no effect. AUX 6 is used for wiper control.

Older receivers (V1200R-PVP and V1300R-PVP) differ slightly from this documentation. Variable speed pan is controlled by AUX 3 and AUX 4, allowing 4 speed levels. Tilt is similarly controlled by AUX 5 and AUX 6.
